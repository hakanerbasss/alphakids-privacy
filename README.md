# Alpha Kids Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

## Overview
Alpha Kids is an educational app designed to help users learn English vocabulary through interactive games. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users.

## Data Collection
Alpha Kids does not collect any personal data from users. The app works fully offline and does not require account registration or login.

## Advertising
This app displays rewarded advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Users choose to watch ads voluntarily to unlock content. AdMob may collect device information for ad delivery purposes. For more information, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Local Storage
The app uses your device's local storage (localStorage) solely to remember which content you have unlocked during the day. This data never leaves your device.

## Third Party Services
- **Google AdMob** — for optional rewarded advertisements
