# Alpha Kids Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

Alpha Kids is an educational app designed for children. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users.

## Data Collection
Alpha Kids does not collect any personal data from users. The app works fully offline and does not require account registration.

## Children Privacy (COPPA)
This app is designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. No data is transmitted to external servers.
